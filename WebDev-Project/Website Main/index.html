Areas of Interest

Machine Learning

My passion for Machine Learning has led me to work on several innovative projects. One of my key projects, Object Detection using CNN, involved developing a model by combining Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) with the You Only Look Once (YOLO) model. To enhance accuracy, I implemented intersection over union and non-max suppression techniques. Another significant project, Image-to-Image Translation using GANs, involved developing a translator using a Pix2Pix GAN. I optimized its performance through rigorous tuning and conducted a comparative analysis with a U-Net model using a dataset of film negatives and their corresponding positive images. I also implemented image super-resolution on the GAN outputs to enhance the final results and streamlined the entire process. Additionally, I worked on fluid dynamics data using Artificial Neural Networks (ANNs), showcasing my ability to apply machine learning techniques to complex scientific data.


Material Science

I have a profound interest in Material Science, which was sparked by taking a comprehensive course in the subject. This course deepened my understanding of the fundamental principles and advanced concepts, driving my enthusiasm to explore this field further.


Design

In the realm of design, I was a key contributor to a team that designed and implemented the first prototype for a multi-utility toothbrush, which is currently under review for patent rights. My contributions included implementing initial designs using Blender and manufacturing prototypes using 3D printing and additive manufacturing techniques. This project highlights my ability to blend creativity with practical engineering skills to bring innovative ideas to life.
